The stuff that gets me inspired

interests

Growing up in the vibrant streets of JP, I was immersed in a world of visual art—from captivating TV shows and immersive comics to expressive graffiti. Conversations with friends about anime and videogames and the typography of local food stalls spiked my creativity and passion for design.

  • Cinema

    The art of visual storytelling in cinema has profoundly shaped my approach to design. It’s where I discovered the impact of composition, color, and movement.

    01

  • Anime/Cartoons

    Anime and cartoons have shaped my approach as an artist and designer, teaching me to use vibrant visuals and dynamic storytelling. They inspire me to create designs that are both imaginative and emotionally resonant.

  • Graphic Novels

    Comic book pages are graphic compositions. Balance, rhythm, and contrast guide layouts, leading readers through stories.Enough said. 03
