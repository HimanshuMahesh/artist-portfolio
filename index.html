The stuff that gets me inspired

interests

growing up in the streets of JP, i was surrounded by visual art almost everywhere. From the captivating TV shows to the immersive comics, and the expressive graffiti on forgotten buildings, it was intresting fosho. The animated discussions with my friends about the latest anime we were into or the typography of those corner food stalls, that’s what sparked my creativity initially.

  • Cinema

    its captivating blend of visual storytelling and motion, has profoundly influenced my passion for design and art. I discovered the power of composition, color, and movement. i have a Letterboxd account.

  • Anime

    Picture this: me, a kid with a bowl of popcorn, sprawled on the living room floor, eyes glued to the TV screen. What was I watching? Dragon Ball Z and Akira, of course! Those colorful characters, epic battles, and tear-jerking moments—pure magic. Little did I know that anime would be my gateway drug into the world of art and illustration.
